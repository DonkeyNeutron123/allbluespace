About Us

Our History

All Blue is a cutting-edge space tourism company that was founded in 2020 by a group of visionary entrepreneurs with a shared passion for space exploration. With a mission to provide unparalleled space travel experiences, All Blue quickly gained a reputation for offering the world's best space tourism.

All Blue founded 2010

"All Blue" is a leading space tourism company that was founded in 2010 with a vision!

First Passenger 2016

In 2016, "All Blue" made history by taking its first passengers on board "Blue Rides" for an unforgettable space tour. This marked a significant milestone in the company's journey towards making space travel accessible to a wider audience.

1 week tour 2018

In 2018, "All Blue" achieved another remarkable feat by allowing passengers to stay on the International Space Station (ISS) for a whole week. This provided a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for customers to experience life in space and witness the wonders of the universe up close.

Biggest Space Tourism Agency 2020

By 2020, "All Blue" had established itself as the biggest space tourism company in the world, with a reputation for delivering unmatched space travel experiences. The company's commitment to innovation, safety, and customer satisfaction had earned it a loyal following of space enthusiasts from around the globe.
